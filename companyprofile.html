Our Story

Established in 2002, we have grown to become one of the premium manufacturers, suppliers, and distributors of advanced industrial CNC routers. Our journey began with a vision to revolutionize the woodworking and stone engraving industry with precision technology.

Under the leadership of our proprietor Mr. Kuldeep Singh, we've built a reputation for quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. With 50 dedicated staff members, including 15 skilled engineers, we produce 45 units monthly to serve clients across India.
